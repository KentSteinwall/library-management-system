RULES ON USE


  1. Readers must produce their belongings such as books, notes etc. to the gatekeepers when they enter to the library and go out of the library.
  2. No bags, cases, parcels, cellular phones, personal copies of books & printed materials, umbrellas etc. should be brought into the Library.
  3. SILENCE should be observed in all public reading areas.
  4. Seats in the Library may not be reserved or removed by the readers.
  5. Readers are not allowed to hold discussions in the library.
  6. Consumption of food and drink, smoking and the use of matches or an open flame are forbidden in the Library.
  7. No readers may enter any part of the Library to which entry is forbidden.
  8. Visitors to the Library are required to obtain the permission of the Librarian to enter the Library.
  9. Any disorderly or improper conduct or breach of regulations will render the reader or borrower concerned liable to suspension from using the library.
  10. Do not leave your personal belongings in front of the library or around the library. Please use the Cloakroom.
  11. Valuable articles or cash should not be kept in the cloakroom. The Library Staff will not hold themselves responsible for any losses.
  12. It is requested that all readers should avoid wearing unsuitable clothes like shorts, caps etc. when they enter the Library.
  13. Prior approval in writing should be obtained form the Librarian to take photographs inside the Library.
  • Library materials should be treated and protected as a National Resource.
  • Please forward your suggestions and complaints to the reader services section.
  • Your corporation is appreciated to maintain an effective information service.
